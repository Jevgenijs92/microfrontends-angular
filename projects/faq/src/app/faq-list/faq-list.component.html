Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

This is a website that allows you to create a list of your favorite movies and share it with your friends.

How do I create a list?

First, you need to create an account. Then, you can create a list by clicking on the "Create List" button on the top right of the page.

How do I add movies to my list?

Once you have created a list, you can add movies to it by searching for them on the "Search" page and clicking on the "Add to List" button.

How do I share my list with my friends?

Once you have created a list, you can share it with your friends by clicking on the "Share" button on the top right of the page.

How do I view my friend's lists?

You can view your friend's lists by clicking on the "Friends" button on the top right of the page.
